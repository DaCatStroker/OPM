MAIN CHARACTERS

SAITAMA

Saitama is the main protagonist of the One Punch Man anime. He has extraordinary strength, speed, and agility and can defeat any villain with just one punch. He is the strongest hero in the world and is a symbol of hope to many people.

GENOS

Genos is a cyborg who joins Saitama in his quest to become a powerful hero. He serves as a mentor and companion to Saitama, helping him hone his skills and become stronger. He is also one of the few people who know Saitama's true identity and is a loyal friend.

LORD BOROS

Lord Boros is the main antagonist of the series and the most powerful villain. He is a powerful alien who seeks to find a worthy opponent to fight and is willing to do anything to achieve this goal. He poses a great threat to the world and is a formidable foe.
